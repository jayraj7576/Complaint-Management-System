# User Guide - Complaint Management System

Welcome to the **JSPM's Complaint Management System**. This guide will help you navigate the portal based on your assigned role.

---

## 👤 For Students & Staff (Users)

### 1. Submitting a Complaint
- Navigate to the **"New Complaint"** section from your dashboard.
- Provide a clear **Title** and detailed **Description**.
- Select the appropriate **Category** (e.g., Infrastructure, Academic).
- Choose a **Priority** level.
- Attach any supporting photos or documents (PDF/JPG/PNG max 5MB).
- Submit to receive a unique **Ticket ID** (e.g., CMP-20250115001).

### 2. Tracking Progress
- View your active tickets in the **"My Complaints"** list.
- Click on a ticket to see the **Real-time Status** (Pending, In Progress, Resolved).
- Read administrative **Remarks** and view the **History Timeline**.

### 3. Profile Management
- Visit the **"Profile"** page to update your contact information or change your account password.
- You can also upload a profile picture for better identification by staff.

---

## 🏢 For Department Heads

### 1. Reviewing Assignments
- Your dashboard shows complaints specifically assigned to your department.
- Review the details and attachments provided by the user.

### 2. Updating Tickets
- Add **Remarks** to clarify issues or request more information.
- Update the **Status** to "In Progress" or "Resolved" once the work is complete.
- All actions are logged in the ticket's history for transparency.

---

## 🛠️ For System Administrators

### 1. Overall Dashboard
- Monitor system-wide statistics: total tickets, resolution rates, and category distribution.
- Identify bottlenecks in specific departments.

### 2. User & Dept Management
- Use the **"User Management"** section to promote users to 'Department Head' or 'Admin' roles.
- Manage the list of active college departments in the **"Settings"** area.

### 3. Reports & Analytics
- Use the **"Reports"** section to generate summaries for specific date ranges.
- Export detailed data to **PDF** or **Excel** for board meetings or accreditation processes.

### 4. System Settings
- Configure the **App Name**, **Support Email**, and **Escalation Rules**.
- Toggle **Maintenance Mode** during system updates.

---

## 🆘 Troubleshooting & Support

- **Forgotten Password**: Contact the System Administrator for a manual reset.
- **Upload Errors**: Ensure your file is under 5MB and is a supported format (JPG, PNG, PDF).
- **Escalation**: If your ticket remains "Pending" for over 48 hours, use the **"Escalate"** button on the ticket detail page.
